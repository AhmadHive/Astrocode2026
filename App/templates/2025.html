01. Official Events & Briefing

The University of Amman organized the "AstroCode: Explore. Code. Launch" competition under the patronage of Prof. Dr. Mohammad Al-Widyan. Supported by the IEEE Student Branch and specialized communities like AESS and IEEE Computer Society, the event simulated global space challenges to empower students in aerospace technology.

A keynote panel titled "Space Development in the Era of Modern Technology" featured experts like Dr. Ammar Skaji, Dr. Hanna Sabat, and Dr. Ahmad Al-Hesban, moderated by Mohammad Abu Amsha.
