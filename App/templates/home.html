Astro Code 2026 is a national, multidisciplinary competition bringing together university students to solve challenges in space science, AI, and software development.

Participants use advanced technology and creative approaches to develop innovative solutions in areas like robotics, data analysis, Artificial Intelligence, health and medicine, Web and App, and space systems.

The competition focuses on tackling real-world challenges in Jordan, turning national issues into practical projects with tangible impact.

> Over 40 teams from more than 15 universities participate, collaborating to implement and present their solutions while gaining hands-on experience and teamwork skills.
